Leo Buscaglia

I loved reading Leo Buscaglia back in the '80s. His words radiated such warmth, caring, and joy. He really seemed to enjoy life and people. And his words were often inspiring.

He nailed the dynamics of learning well: it happens through curiosity and is intimately connected to play.

"It is paradoxical that many educators and parents still differentiate between a time for learning and a time for play without seeing the vital connection between them." ~ Leo Buscaglia
