"I have a need...for cool, verdant places...beneath the trees...secret empty spaces..."
Mary Chapin Carpenter

Nature photography both invigorates and refreshes me. It allows me to escape the harried pace of life...trekking through woods or meandering through gardens. My focus narrows to the beauty around me. As I go deeper into nature, the minutes and hours tick away, the pace of my thoughts slows down. Time slows down. My breathing synchs with the world. With the rhythms of nature. Silence is a balm to my soul...the secret empty spaces of nature that I seek paradoxically refill the places in me that are drained outside of the cool, verdant places of nature.
